## Pricing Rationale

**Market context:** AI consulting rates range from $150–$400/hour for specialized work.

- **Tier 1 ($3–5K/month):** ~$200/hr equivalent (ongoing production labor, fully automated)
- **Tier 2 ($15–25K project):** ~$250–300/hr for design + build + knowledge transfer
- **Tier 3 ($6–10K/month):** ~$300–350/hr equivalent (24/7 on-call, continuous optimization)

Our pricing is conservative for the market. **Justification:**
- We deliver production systems, not reports
- Tier 1 scales (marginal cost of adding a client is near-zero)
- Tier 2 is fixed-scope, fixed-price (no surprise cost overruns)
- Tier 3 includes ongoing optimization (most firms charge separately for this)

## Scope Carveouts — What We Don't Do

We maintain clear boundaries to keep work focused and prevent scope creep:

1. **We don't spend your money**
   - No API subscriptions, data feeds, cloud infrastructure billed to you
   - You own or contract those directly
   - We integrate with your existing tools

2. **We don't handle personal/sensitive data**
   - No PII, medical records, social security numbers
   - No data covered by HIPAA, GDPR without explicit agreement
   - All systems designed for financial market data (public + proprietary)

3. **We don't make investment decisions**
   - We generate signals and recommendations; you decide
   - All output is decision-support, not financial advice
   - No fiduciary relationships

4. **We don't handle compliance/licensing**
   - Signal generation ≠ investment advisor registration
   - Your firm is responsible for regulatory classification
   - We can help with documentation but not legal liability

5. **We don't do custom UI/web apps**
   - Dashboards delivered via Slack, Discord, CSV/JSON exports
   - If you want custom web UI, that's a separate project (Tier 2 variant)

6. **We don't guarantee returns**
   - Backtests are historical, not forward-looking
   - Market conditions change; signals degrade over time
   - We monitor for degradation but can't prevent it
